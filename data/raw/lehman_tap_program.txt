TUITION ASSISTANCE PROGRAM (TAP) — LEHMAN COLLEGE

IMPORTANT NOTE (2026-27): You must submit the 2026-2027 FAFSA before your TAP application can be processed.

Lehman College TAP CODE: 1412

TAP is an entitlement program. Awards are noncompetitive — any student who meets eligibility criteria will receive funds. All applicants must be a New York State resident and an eligible U.S. citizen or permanent resident alien. Apply annually to HESC (New York State Higher Education Services Corporation).

PART-TIME TAP:
Allows partial TAP payments for students taking 6 to 11 credits. Requirements:
- Be a New York State resident
- Be admitted into a degree program
- Maintain a cumulative GPA of 2.0 or higher
- Register for at least 6 credit hours, but less than 12 credits
- Maintain good academic standing

TAP QUALIFICATIONS (Full-Time):
- Registered for at least 12 credits (15 credits recommended)
- Winter courses CANNOT be used toward full-time enrollment for Spring TAP eligibility
- Credits counted must meet degree requirements
- Must complete a specific number of credits in the previous semester
- Must accumulate a specific number of credits toward your degree
- Must maintain a specific minimum GPA
- Must declare a major by the time you complete 60 credits

NOTE: SEEK students may receive TAP awards for 10 semesters; all other undergraduates for 8 semesters.

TAP ELIGIBILITY CHARTS:
For students who received FIRST TAP award in SUMMER 2006 or later (Non-SEEK and SEEK):
Payment 1: 0 credits completed, 0 accumulated, GPA 0
Payment 2: 6 credits completed, 3 accumulated, GPA 1.1
Payment 3: 6 credits completed, 9 accumulated, GPA 1.2
Payment 4: 9 credits completed, 21 accumulated, GPA 1.3
Payment 5: 9 credits completed, 33 accumulated, GPA 2.0
Payment 6: 12 credits completed, 45 accumulated, GPA 2.0
Payment 7: 12 credits completed, 60 accumulated, GPA 2.0
Payment 8: 12 credits completed, 75 accumulated, GPA 2.0

TAP INELIGIBILITY — You may lose TAP eligibility if you:
- Have accumulated 60 credits and have not declared a major
- Are repeating a course that cannot be used toward full-time enrollment for TAP eligibility
- Failed to complete the required credits in your last TAP-supported semester
- Have not earned the required credits for your specific TAP payment number
- Do not have the required GPA for this payment

REPEATED COURSES:
Courses where you already received a passing grade CANNOT be counted toward TAP full-time study requirements. Exceptions: repeating a failed course, or repeating a course where the passing grade is unacceptable for your specific curriculum.

SUMMER TAP AWARDS — Requirements:
- Registered full time in the prior Spring semester
- Earned at least 24 credits from the same institution in the prior two terms
- Enrolled part time for at least 6, but fewer than 12 semester hours
NOTE: Transferred credits cannot be included in the 24-credit requirement.

ONE-TIME TAP WAIVER:
Students who fail to maintain good academic standing may apply for a one-time TAP waiver for these extenuating circumstances ONLY:
- Personal illness involving hospitalization or extended home confinement
- Illness in the immediate family requiring extended absence from classes
- Emotionally disabling condition that prevented attending classes
- Military duty, incarceration, or other government agency involvement

NOTE: Normal family responsibilities, work, or fear of failing a class do NOT qualify.
To apply: email TAP.Audit@Lehman.cuny.edu — all waivers must include documentation.

ADA PART-TIME TAP:
ADA Law allows certain students with disability limitations to access TAP while attending part time. Students must complete all credits attempted each semester.
