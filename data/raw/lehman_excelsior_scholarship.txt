EXCELSIOR SCHOLARSHIP — LEHMAN COLLEGE

The Excelsior Scholarship is a New York State funded grant program that, when combined with other grants/scholarships, allows eligible full-time students to attend Lehman College tuition free.

IMPORTANT NOTE: The NYS Excelsior Scholarship application for SPRING 2026 is now CLOSED. The deadline was February 3, 2026. To apply in the future, visit hesc.ny.gov/excelsior

ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS:
- Be a NYS resident and U.S. citizen or permanent resident
- Have graduated from a U.S. high school, earned a High School Equivalency diploma, or passed the ATB Exam
- Household income at or below $125,000 (for 2024-2025 academic year, based on 2022 tax return)
- Enrolled as a full-time student — at least 12 credits (15 credits recommended)
- Complete at least 30 credits per Academic Year
- Plan to reside in NYS following graduation for the length of time the award was received
- If employed after graduation, must be employed in NYS

IMPORTANT CREDIT NOTE:
- Winter courses CANNOT be used toward full-time enrollment for Spring Excelsior eligibility
- Winter courses CANNOT be used to determine Good Academic Standing for the Spring term

HOW TO APPLY:
1. Complete the FAFSA at studentaid.gov
2. Complete the NYS TAP application at hesc.ny.gov
3. Upon completing TAP, you will be prompted to complete the Excelsior Scholarship application
