2026-27 FAFSA AND TAP APPLICATIONS — NOW OPEN

The 2026-27 Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) and the NYS Tuition Assistance Program (TAP) applications are open. Submitting your applications early helps you maximize financial aid opportunities.

WHAT YOU NEED TO DO:
1. Gather all necessary documents and create your StudentAid.gov Account
2. Complete your FAFSA application at studentaid.gov
3. Complete your TAP application using the state application link at the end of the FAFSA

WHY APPLY EARLY?
- You'll know your aid eligibility sooner
- Gives you time to correct unexpected errors or delays
- Colleges use this information to build your financial aid package

NYS DREAM ACT / ALTERNATE PATHWAYS:
Students who don't meet citizenship or residency requirements may still qualify for New York State financial aid through the Senator José Peralta NYS DREAM Act or the Alternate Eligibility Path.

HESC VIRTUAL HELP EVENTS:
HESC hosts recurring virtual FAFSA/TAP completion events. Visit hesc.ny.gov/events for current schedule.
