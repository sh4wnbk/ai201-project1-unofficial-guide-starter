HESC STUDENT UPDATE — FEBRUARY 2026

STAY ON TOP OF YOUR FINANCIAL AID:
Don't let small application mistakes delay your financial aid! Missing signatures, incorrect income details, or incomplete information can slow the process. Log in to your FAFSA and TAP regularly, review your applications, and correct any errors promptly.

EXCELSIOR SCHOLARSHIP — SPRING 2026:
If you're starting college in spring 2026, or if you're already enrolled and missed the fall Excelsior application, you may still qualify for free tuition at state-operated SUNY or CUNY colleges through the Excelsior Scholarship. The deadline for applying was February 3, 2026.

TIPS FOR CUNY/SUNY STUDENTS:
- Apply for FAFSA and TAP as early as possible — October 1 is when the new year opens
- After completing your FAFSA, use the link at the end to apply for TAP
- Log in to your HESC Account regularly to check for errors or updates
- Fix any issues quickly to prevent delays in receiving your aid
- If you haven't submitted your 2025-26 FAFSA and TAP applications, there's still time to apply

NYS DREAM ACT / ALTERNATE ELIGIBILITY:
Students who don't meet citizenship or residency requirements may still qualify for New York State financial aid through alternate pathways, like the NYS DREAM Act or the Alternate Eligibility Path. Visit the Applying for Aid webpage to learn more.
