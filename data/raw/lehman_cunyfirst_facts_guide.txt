CUNYFIRST AND FACTS — HOW TO CHECK YOUR FINANCIAL AID AT LEHMAN

WHAT IS CUNYFIRST?
CUNYfirst (home.cunyfirst.cuny.edu) is the official student portal for all CUNY colleges including Lehman. It is the authoritative source for your financial aid information — all official financial aid records are stored here.

HOW TO VIEW YOUR FINANCIAL AID AWARD IN CUNYFIRST:
1. Log on to CUNYfirst at home.cunyfirst.cuny.edu
2. Click on Student Center
3. Click on Financial Aid
4. Click the correct Aid Year (covers Summer, Fall, and Spring of that academic year)
5. Review your Award Summary

YOUR TO DO LIST IN CUNYFIRST:
Your CUNYfirst TO DO list alerts you to any documentation or action required by the Financial Aid Office. Check it regularly. Common alerts include:
- Verification documents required
- Missing signatures or information
- Identity confirmation needed
- Additional documentation for special circumstances

If you see items on your TO DO list, submit the required documents to the Financial Aid Office as soon as possible — unresolved items delay your aid disbursement.

WHAT IS FACTS?
FACTS (Financial Aid Eligibility & Certification Tracking System) is a web-based advisory tool accessed through CUNYfirst that helps you track your eligibility for:
- Tuition Assistance Program (TAP)
- Aid for Part-Time Study (APTS)
- Selected HESC scholarships

IMPORTANT: FACTS is NOT your official financial aid record. It is an advisory tool only. Your official record is in CUNYfirst.

HOW TO ACCESS FACTS:
1. Go to home.cunyfirst.cuny.edu
2. Click on Student Center
3. Click on the DegreeWorks and FACTS tile
4. Under Lehman College, click on the FACTS link
5. Press "I Agree" on the disclaimer
6. You will be directed to the FACTS Home Page

FACTS FEATURES:
- Current Eligibility Tab: Shows your current TAP eligibility status and past aid history. Includes a detailed summary of your current eligibility.
- FACTS Transcript Tab: Shows current and past eligibility status for State Aid, including previously registered courses.

IMPORTANT TIMING NOTE:
When changes are made to CUNYfirst or Schedule Builder, it typically takes 24-72 hours to reflect in the FACTS audit. Changes that trigger updates include:
- Newly registered courses
- Grade changes
- Changes to transfer credits
- Changes to majors, concentrations, or minors
